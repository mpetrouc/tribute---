Director David Fincher

"Entertainment has to come hand in hand with a little bit of medicine. Some people go to the movies to be reminded that everything's okay. I don't make those kinds of movies. That, to me, is a lie. Everything's not okay." - David Fincher"

This is a tribute to the famous movie director David Fincher.He is known for his stylish,dark-themed movies with intelligent plot. What makes him stand-out is his atmospheric cinematography and great character development.

Jump to Bottom
  • He was born on August 28th of 1962 in Denver, Colorado.
  • He decided he wanted to be a director after watching Steven Spielberg's films.
  • His first work experience in the field was first with John Korty and then with George Lucas who was his neighboor.
  • He was the director of many popular music videos of the '80s' and '90s' like Aerosmith's 'Janie's got a gun' and Madonna's 'Vogue'.
  • In 1992 he debuted with the film 'Alien 3'.Even though it received poor critiques it served as a valuable experience for his next works.
  • His next films all received great critical and commercial success.In chronogical order:Se7en(1995),The Game(1997),Fight Club(1999),Panic Room(2002),Zodiac(2007),The Social Network(2010),The Girl with the Dragon Tatoo(2011) and Gone Girl(2014).
  • He also worked on tv-series such as 'House of Cards' in 2013 and "Mindhunter"in 2017-2018.
  • He is currently working on the film 'World War Z 2' which is said to be released in 2019.
